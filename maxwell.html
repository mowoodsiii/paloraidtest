MAXWELL Small Sat Mission (UNP)

The objective of this project is to mature CubeSat communications technology through a flight demonstration. The key technologies we plan to demonstrate are:

  • High rate (20Mbps) data downlink at X-band (~8300MHz)
  • Spread spectrum Code Division Multiple Access (CDMA) 2Mbps data downlink at X-band (~8300MHz)
  • 200kbps S-band uplink (~2250MHz)
  • Demo of high gain (>20dBi) X-band reflectarray antenna
Our stretch goals include:
  • High rate (50Mbps) data downlink at X-band (~8300MHz)
  • Spread spectrum Code Division Multiple Access (CDMA) 20kbps S-band uplink (~2250MHz)
  • Full on-orbit characterization of reflectarray antenna
