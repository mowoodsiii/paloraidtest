Welcome to paloraid...

This is the orientation and directory site for the CU Small Satellite research group, hosted on the paloraid (CCAR) server at CU Boulder. This page is intended for students involved in CubeSat missions as a part of the graduate projects course offered by the University of Colorado at Boulder department of AeroSpace Engineering and serves as a directory for participants to find orientation instructions, course materials, and project-based documents. (Please note that this site is intentionally generalized to be applied to all CU CusbeSat missions, past or present.

Please contact the Graduate Projects course coordinator, your project manager/team, or Dr. Scott Palo, for more specific details)
