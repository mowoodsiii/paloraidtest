Challenger Small Sat Mission (QB50)

QB50 is a part of an international effort to launch a constellation of 50 CubeSats into orbit to study Earth's thermosphere. Funded by the National Science Foundation, CU's QB50 contribution - known as "Challenger" - will carry an Ion-Neutral Mass Spectrometer to measure the constituents of Earth's thermosphere, and their relative abundance.

The data from the 50 CubeSats of the QB50 mission will be compiled in a common repository that will be used for coordinated, international scientific analysis.
